Clients can record feedback in a format that is way better than email. Better feedback leads to an improved collaboration experience and minimizes needed revisions.

Next-level Proofing Tools

When you send work. clients can provide text-based feedback (just like an email), or a recording of their feedback. Voive and annotation recording provides a much richer feedack experience that is easier to give and much more informative to receive. Try our feedback tools for an enriching and time-saving collaborative experience.

Recorded Voice

Hearing pauses, tone of voice, hesitation, excitement, and delight provides way more information than in a wall of a text. Relive clients from trying to figure out how to express their feedback in words through an offer a more natural feedback approch.

Annotations
